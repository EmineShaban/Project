Charity meetings!

Welcome to the Charity Meetings.

Let me tell you what the idea is. Our main goal is to try to bring goodness and positive emotions into people's lives. We decided to combine the idea of charity and organizing pleasant evenings for people.

Let me tell you about it in more detail and how exactly it works. On our website you can meet a large number of interesting people who will be happy to keep you company for a walk, playing sports or going to the cinema. By selecting the "All meetings" tab, you can see the candidates that are currently available. Next, you need to click on the candidate you are interested in and if you see the "Join" button in this tab, it’s mean that you can meet with this person. An important condition is that when you choose the person and pressed button “Join” you must transfer 10 dollars to a charitable organisation which selected in “Charity” page. In this section you can find organisations with which we keep in touch and help them in every possible way.

If you want to be the candidate that people will choose, you need to register on our site, and then go to the “Create meeting" tab. Any user can take part in charity meetings. You can create a meeting at any time and our team is sure that thanks to these meetings you not only help charities, but also share your experience with other people and it is also a good way to make new friends!
