FAQ

1Why I can not create a meeting?

In order to create a meeting, you need to register on our website, after that, you you need to fill in information about meeting at "Create meetings" section. Make sure that you enter the correct information. If you have any difficulties, please write to us at this mail "charitMeetings@gmail.com".

2What do they do if the guest does not come to the meeting?

First of all, you should not worry, force majeure circumstances do not depend on us. If within 20 minutes, your guest did not come, please write to us at this mail "charitMeetings@gmail.com", within 5-10 minutes we will give you feedback and do our best so that your next evening does not disappoint you!
